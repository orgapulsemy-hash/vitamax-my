Refund Policy

VitaMAX.MY · Last updated: May 2025 · vitamax.my
🔄 Short version: We offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee on your first order of any product. No questions asked. No need to return the product.

1. Our 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand completely behind the quality of every VitaMAX product. If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason within 30 days of receiving your order, we will refund your full purchase price — no questions asked.

This guarantee applies to:

  • Your first order of any single product
  • Any order where the product arrived damaged or defective
  • Any order that did not arrive within 30 days of the estimated delivery date

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us within 30 days of receiving your order:

  • Email: orgapulse.my@gmail.com
  • WhatsApp: Message us directly

Please include your order number (found in your confirmation email), your name, and the reason for your refund request. We will process your refund within 3–5 business days.

3. Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method:

  • Credit/debit card: Refunded to your card via Stripe. Appears within 5–10 business days depending on your bank.
  • PayPal: Refunded to your PayPal account within 3–5 business days.
  • WhatsApp/bank transfer orders: Refunded via bank transfer or preferred method.

4. Exchanges

If you received the wrong product or a damaged item, we will send a replacement immediately at no cost to you. Contact us via WhatsApp or email with a photo of the issue and your order number.

5. Shipping Costs

We do not refund shipping costs unless the return is due to our error (wrong item sent, damaged product). Express shipping upgrades are non-refundable.

6. Wholesale Orders

Wholesale and bulk orders (carton quantities) are not covered by the 30-day money-back guarantee unless the products are defective or damaged on arrival. Please inspect all wholesale orders within 7 days of receipt and report any issues immediately.

7. Contact Us

For any questions about returns or refunds:
Email: orgapulse.my@gmail.com
WhatsApp: WhatsApp Us
